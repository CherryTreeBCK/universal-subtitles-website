Help Section

My subtitles are not updating. What should I do?
Check if your speakers are powered on. The program cannot detect audio if no output device is connected.
How can I view the transcription log?
All transcriptions are logged in the terminal window that opens alongside the program.
What are the controls?
To open the settings menu, right click the overlay. The default controls are F9 to show the overlay and F10 to hide it.
